Buying Requirements

Financial Preparation

  • Pre-approval letter from lender
  • Proof of funds for down payment
  • Recent bank statements (2-3 months)
  • W-2 forms and tax returns

Property Preferences

  • Desired neighborhoods and areas
  • Budget range and price limits
  • Must-have features and amenities
  • Timeline for purchase

Personal Information

  • Valid government ID
  • Employment verification
  • Credit report authorization
  • Contact information and availability

Next Steps

  • Schedule consultation with Francisco
  • Property search and viewings
  • Offer preparation and negotiation
  • Closing process and finalization

Selling Requirements

Property Information

  • Property address and details
  • Current mortgage information
  • Property tax records
  • Homeowners insurance details

Property Condition

  • Recent repairs and improvements
  • Known issues or needed repairs
  • Property photos and videos
  • Utility and maintenance records

Financial Details

  • Desired sale price range
  • Timeline for selling
  • Flexibility on price and terms
  • Plans after selling (buying, renting, etc.)

Marketing Strategy

  • Professional photography and staging
  • MLS listing and online marketing
  • Open houses and private showings
  • Offer review and negotiation
